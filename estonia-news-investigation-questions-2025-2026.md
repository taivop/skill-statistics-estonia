# Estonia News Investigation Questions (Feb 2025 to Feb 2026)

These are candidate investigations tied to major Estonia news themes from the last year, designed for agent workflows that use this repo's public-source skills.

1. How did Baltic grid synchronization (Feb 2025) change Estonia's hourly electricity price volatility and import structure?
   - Likely sources: `sources/energy-data`, `sources/statistics-api`, `sources/weather-data`
2. Did undersea cable incidents (Estlink/telecom) materially affect energy reliability and balancing costs?
   - Likely sources: `sources/energy-data`, `sources/public-finance-data`, `sources/procurement-data`
3. Which sectors benefit most from Estonia's renewable fee cap for energy-intensive firms, and with what fiscal cost?
   - Likely sources: `sources/energy-data`, `sources/public-finance-data`, `sources/business-register-open-data`
   - Source certainty: medium
4. Is Estonia's planned defense-spending increase (5%+ GDP direction) visible in procurement pipelines and contract concentration?
   - Likely sources: `sources/public-finance-data`, `sources/procurement-data`, `sources/state-assets-register`
5. Which ministries/agencies are driving the largest year-on-year spending growth in the 2025->2026 budget cycle?
   - Likely sources: `sources/public-finance-data`, `sources/government-action-programme`
6. How much of 2025 tax-revenue growth came from VAT/tax-rate changes versus real economic activity?
   - Likely sources: `sources/tax-customs-data`, `sources/statistics-api`, `sources/public-finance-data`
7. What behavioral effects followed the new motor vehicle tax (registrations, vehicle age/emissions mix, import patterns)?
   - Likely sources: `sources/vehicle-background-check`, `sources/transport-traffic-data`, `sources/tax-customs-data`
   - Source certainty: medium
8. In 2025 local elections, where did turnout rise most and how did e-vote vs paper-vote shares shift by municipality?
   - Likely sources: `sources/election-results-data`, `sources/local-council-volis`
9. Did local-election outcomes translate into measurable policy/budget shifts in Tallinn and Tartu within 3-6 months?
   - Likely sources: `sources/tallinn-council-documents`, `sources/tartu-document-register`, `sources/local-government-finance-benchmarks`
10. What changed in voting-rights composition (for example, third-country nationals) and how might it have altered district representation?
    - Likely sources: `sources/election-results-data`, `sources/local-council-volis`
    - Source certainty: lower (may need additional legal/population datasets)
11. Are youth-unemployment increases concentrated in specific counties and linked to sectoral contraction?
    - Likely sources: `sources/unemployment-statistics`, `sources/statistics-api`
12. Did home-price growth in 2025 correlate with permit issuance/completions and municipal planning decisions?
    - Likely sources: `sources/construction-register`, `sources/planning-decisions`, `sources/statistics-api`
13. Is teacher shortage severity associated with municipal financing patterns and demographic pressure?
    - Likely sources: `sources/education-data`, `sources/local-government-finance-benchmarks`, `sources/statistics-api`
14. How did healthcare demand, waiting pressure, and spending move in 2025 by specialty/region?
    - Likely sources: `sources/health-statistics`, `sources/health-insurance-fund-reports`, `sources/health-welfare-open-data`
15. Did labour-inspectorate enforcement intensity change in sectors with higher layoffs/unemployment?
    - Likely sources: `sources/labour-inspectorate-enforcement`, `sources/labour-inspectorate-statistics`, `sources/unemployment-statistics`
16. Is cyber-incident growth (fraud/phishing/service disruption) concentrated in specific sectors and months?
    - Likely sources: `sources/cyber-incidents-cert-ee`, `sources/x-road-usage-statistics`, `sources/public-sector-it-systems-riha`
17. After major cyber/fraud waves, did government agencies publish measurable mitigation actions and service-hardening outputs?
    - Likely sources: `sources/cyber-incidents-cert-ee`, `sources/government-office-document-register`, `sources/ministry-document-registries`
18. e-Residency hit record impact in 2025: which origin countries and company types drive tax contribution persistence after year 1?
    - Likely sources: `sources/e-residency-dashboard`, `sources/business-register-open-data`, `sources/tax-customs-data`
19. Did security incidents linked to foreign sabotage correspond with legal outcomes, policing patterns, or sentencing trends?
    - Likely sources: `sources/court-proceedings-data`, `sources/supreme-court-judgments`, `sources/police-operational-statistics`
    - Source certainty: medium/lower for direct sabotage tagging
20. Rail Baltica and other strategic infrastructure: are timelines, permits, and contract amendments consistent with public commitments?
    - Likely sources: `sources/procurement-data`, `sources/planning-decisions`, `sources/environmental-permit-decisions`, `sources/public-finance-data`
    - Source certainty: medium
